Mail Victory is a blog about email development with an emphasis on simplifying building responsive emails. Mail Victory developed Mailmods [HTML Modules]: A Responsive Email Templating Framework.



Mailmods were created to streamline email development so that you, the email creator (developer), can focus on great content and beautiful creative.

Mailmods are built to make for seamless email development. They process was started over a year ago with the task of making a mobile friendly newsletter. In the process it started to be easier to make email parts and putting them into a basic HTML Skeleton.

After streamlining the basic HTML Skeleton and Module parts of a responsive email it became clear that using a pre-processor could speed up and simplify the responsive email development process even more.

Jade, HAML and SCSS can be incorporated into your project to further simplify the development time and process.
